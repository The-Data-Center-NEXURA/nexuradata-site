Personal information protection

Privacy policy

This policy explains, in clear terms, how NEXURADATA intends to collect, use, retain and protect personal information communicated through the site or through an intake request.

1. Scope

This policy explains in general terms how NEXURADATA handles personal information shared through its website, by email or as part of a business intake process.

2. Information collected

NEXURADATA may collect only the information necessary to process a request, including:

  • Name, company and contact details.
  • Details related to a service request or technical incident.
  • Information needed for administrative, contractual or legal follow-up.

3. Use

The information is used only to:

  • Respond to requests and assess client needs.
  • Deliver, administer and improve the services offered.
  • Comply with legal, contractual and security obligations.

4. Disclosure and internal access

Access to information should be limited to the people who need it to process a file, ensure administrative follow-up, comply with a legal obligation or protect legitimate interests related to the service provided.

5. Retention and protection

Personal information is retained for the period reasonably necessary for the purposes for which it was collected. Appropriate administrative, technical and organizational safeguards should be applied to protect confidentiality.

6. Privacy incident

If an incident affects personal information, NEXURADATA must assess it promptly, take the steps needed to reduce risk and act in accordance with applicable obligations, including notification when required.

7. Your rights

Subject to applicable law, an individual may request access to, correction of or withdrawal of certain personal information held about them.

8. Privacy requests

For any request relating to privacy, access, correction or the protection of personal information, use the following address: contact@nexuradata.ca.

The named privacy contact is added at official site activation and carried into applicable contractual documents.
