Frequently asked questions

Pricing and terms

Is the initial assessment really free?

Yes. Every case starts with a no-charge examination of the media. No fees are incurred until you approve the written quote.

What if the recovery fails?

If no usable data can be extracted, you are not charged for the intervention itself. Shipping and material costs remain your responsibility.

Are the listed prices final?

No — these are indicative prices. The final price is always confirmed in writing in the quote before any authorization from you.

Do you accept credit card payment?

Yes. We accept credit cards, Interac and bank transfers. Payment terms are confirmed in the quote.

Are there any hidden fees?

No. Applicable taxes, shipping costs and any required deposits are all detailed in the written quote. No fees are added after the fact.
