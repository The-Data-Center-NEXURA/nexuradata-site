What these prices mean

  • They provide a public benchmark for the most frequent requests.
  • They do not replace media assessment or the formal quote.
  • They may vary based on actual media condition, urgency and prior attempts.

The prices shown on this page are provided as indicative figures for priority case types and do not replace a technical assessment of the device or actual situation. The final price, applicable taxes, delivery or courier fees where relevant, any required deposits and the payment terms are confirmed in writing before any final authorization or payment.

How the initial assessment works

  1. You describe the device, symptoms, urgency and prior attempts.
  2. NEXURADATA qualifies the immediate risk and the actions to avoid.
  3. The device is received or documented according to the case sensitivity.
  4. A written quote confirms price, known limits and likely timeline.
  5. You authorize the intervention before any billable work begins.
  6. Follow-up continues through the client portal, email or agreed channel.

After quote acceptance

  • Scope is confirmed.
  • Steps and timelines are clarified.
  • Fees, deposits and payment terms are established.
  • Confidentiality and data return framework are agreed.

Frequently asked questions

Pricing and terms

Is the initial assessment really free?

Yes. Every case starts with a no-charge examination of the media. No fees are incurred until you approve the written quote.

What if the recovery fails?

If no usable data can be extracted, you are not charged for the intervention itself. Shipping and material costs remain your responsibility.

Are the listed prices final?

No — these are indicative prices. The final price is always confirmed in writing in the quote before any authorization from you.

Do you accept credit card payment?

Yes. We accept credit cards, Interac and bank transfers. Payment terms are confirmed in the quote.

Are there any hidden fees?

No. Applicable taxes, shipping costs and any required deposits are all detailed in the written quote. No fees are added after the fact.
