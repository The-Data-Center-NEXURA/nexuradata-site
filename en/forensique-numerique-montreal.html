Investigation

Digital forensics in Montreal

This service applies when the question is not only whether files can be recovered, but also what happened, what needs to be preserved and how the facts should be documented.

Frequent scenarios

  • Internal incident, fraud or suspicious activity on a workstation or account.
  • Need to preserve a device or account before review.
  • Ransomware, compromise or suspected exfiltration.
  • Cases that require a clear chronology of events.

What decision-makers usually expect

  • Preservation of the device and traceability of the file.
  • Structured analysis and understandable reporting.
  • Usable material for internal review, counsel or insurer.
  • Clear communication with IT, legal, HR or risk stakeholders.

When a forensic approach matters

It becomes necessary when evidence integrity, event reconstruction or formal documentation matters as much as the technical work itself.

Who this service is for

  • Law firms and outside experts.
  • SMBs and enterprises facing a sensitive incident.
  • Insurers and risk managers.
  • Internal IT and cybersecurity teams.
