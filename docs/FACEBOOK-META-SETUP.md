## 1. Create or claim the Facebook Page

1. Go to https://business.facebook.com/ or https://www.facebook.com/pages/create.
2. Create page name: `NEXURADATA`.
3. Category: `Data Recovery Service`.
4. Secondary categories if available:
   - `Computer Repair Service`
   - `Computer Forensics Service`
   - `Information Technology Company`
5. Website: `https://nexuradata.ca/`.
6. Email: `contact@nexuradata.ca`.
7. Phone: use the confirmed public lab phone only.
8. Location mode:
   - If the lab address is public: add the business address.
   - If the address should remain private: use service-area business settings and list served cities.

## 6. Meta Business Suite

After page creation:

1. Add the page to Meta Business Suite.
2. Add at least two admins controlled by the business.
3. Enable two-factor authentication for admins.
4. Set page notifications to an actively monitored inbox.
5. Connect Instagram only if the account will be maintained.
6. Do not launch ads until the website, phone, email and Google Business Profile are stable.

## 7. Verification and security

Minimum security requirements:

- Business-owned admin accounts only.
- Two-factor authentication required.
- No shared personal password.
- Remove any temporary setup user after launch.
- Review page roles monthly.

Optional later:

- Meta Business verification.
- WhatsApp Business connection.
- Meta Pixel only if a consent banner and privacy update are implemented.
