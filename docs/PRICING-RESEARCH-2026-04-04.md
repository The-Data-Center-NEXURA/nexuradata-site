## Market reading

The market splits into two visible bands:

1. Simple or adjacent services
- Basic transfer / readable support / simple software case
- Rough public band observed: 49,99$ to 129$

2. Real lab recovery
- Logical or specialist recovery on HDD / SSD
- Rough public band observed: 200$ to 700$ on the low/mid side, then much higher for serious physical damage

3. Complex recovery
- Mechanical damage, RAID, NAS, server, enterprise, forensic
- Usually quoted after evaluation, often with no reliable public fixed price

## Recommended NEXURADATA public display

Use a conversion-first structure:

- Deleted files: starting at 79$
- External drive / USB / memory card simple case: starting at 129$
- Phone / personal media simple case: starting at 149$
- HDD / SSD intermediate logical case: starting at 350$
- RAID / NAS / Server: contact us
- Mechanical failure / severe damage: contact us
- Forensic / sensitive / enterprise-critical: contact us

## Why this structure

- It stays competitive with low-end local visible pricing
- It keeps simple profitable cases accessible
- It avoids publishing misleading fixed prices for complex work
- It matches how specialist competitors actually sell the difficult cases
